About the hotel

Welcome to Hotel Malnad! Nestled in the heart of Sirsi, Karnataka, we offer an authentic dining experience that showcases the rich flavors of our region. Enjoy our diverse menu, warm ambiance, and friendly service. Whether you're here for a casual meal or a special occasion, we promise a memorable experience. Come taste the best of Karnataka!

Our Values

At Hotel Malnad, we believe in providing exceptional service, using the freshest ingredients, and creating a welcoming atmosphere for all our guests. Our commitment to quality and authenticity is at the heart of everything we do.

Step into the dining hall of Hotel Malnad, where elegance meets comfort. Our spacious and beautifully decorated dining area is designed to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere for all our guests. Whether you're here for a family gathering, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, our dining hall sets the perfect stage for a memorable experience.
